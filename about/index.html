About Me

I’m currently completing a PhD in physics from the University of Tennessee, Knoxille, studying high-intensity proton beam dynamics as part of the Accelerator Physics group at the Spallation Neutron Source (SNS). I previously obtained a BS in physics from Wheaton College. This blog is a space for me to explore technical concepts; all my posts are categorized here.
